How Custom Pizza Boxes Come Together

The variables to specify when you send a brief: size, board, print, and volume. The right combination depends on your delivery model and how much you want to spend on the wow.

01

Sizes

Standard 9-inch through 16-inch, with 10-inch, 12-inch and 14-inch being the most common runs. Bespoke sizes available where volume justifies a custom die.

02

Board

White board for a clean, print-forward finish. Kraft/brown for a natural, artisan look. Corrugated when delivery robustness matters more than aesthetics.

03

Print

One-colour logo, two-colour, or full-wrap CMYK. Print quality steps up with quantity — entry-level runs are logo-only, higher volumes unlock full-wrap.

04

Sustainability

Recyclable board is standard. Compostable-certified options available where sourcing allows — tell us if this is a brand promise.

05

Volumes & MOQs

Custom printed runs carry a higher minimum than plain stock — printing only makes sense at volume. Send your rough monthly usage and we'll confirm the minimum for your spec.

Custom pizza boxes — details

Custom Pizza Boxes with Your Logo

Custom pizza boxes with your logo are the single most visible branding investment a pizzeria or delivery kitchen can make — the box travels with every delivery, sits on every kitchen counter, and lands in customers' hands. Printed pizza boxes turn the delivery moment into a brand moment, especially critical for delivery-first businesses whose only physical brand touchpoint is the packaging.

Pizza Boxes Wholesale — UK Trade Supply

We work at trade / wholesale volumes for UK pizza businesses — bulk orders per print run, direct trade pricing, one point of contact for the whole order from artwork through delivery. UK-wide fulfilment, including London where most of our pizza-side customers are based.

Pizza Boxes for Delivery Kitchens & Food Trucks

Delivery kitchens and dark kitchens are the growth end of the pizza market — brand-defined only through the packaging that lands on the customer's doorstep. Food trucks and pop-ups have the same problem in reverse: they're at the customer's location once and won't be back. In both, custom printed pizza boxes work harder per pound than any other single marketing investment.

Compostable & Recyclable Pizza Box Options

Recyclable pizza boxes are effectively the default — most corrugated and flat board is kerbside-recyclable in the UK (bearing in mind grease-contamination caveats, which affect all pizza boxes regardless of supplier). Compostable-certified pizza boxes are also available where sourcing supports it — flag this at the enquiry stage so we know it's a brand promise you're making.

Custom Pizza Box Print — Artwork & Approval Process

The standard process: send us your artwork (AI, PDF or high-res PNG is fine) plus the size, board and volume you're planning; we come back with pricing and a rough lead time; you approve a printed proof before the run begins; we produce and deliver. If your artwork needs adjusting for print (colour changes, resolution, positioning) we'll flag it at the quote stage, not surprise you at production.

Who Orders Custom Pizza Boxes Through Us

UK pizza businesses buying custom-printed boxes at trade volumes and wanting a single trade contact rather than juggling artwork briefs across print vendors. If you're running a delivery-focused kitchen or a growing pizzeria, this is what we're set up for.
