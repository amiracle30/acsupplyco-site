Bakery Packaging Sourced to Your Requirement

AC Supply Co. supplies UK bakeries with the boxes, bags and boards their products go out in — sourced directly at trade prices. Bakery packaging is unusually spec-sensitive: a cake box that's 10mm short ruins the product, and a greaseproof that's wrong lets butter through the bag by the time it reaches the customer. So we source against your actual products and volumes rather than pushing a fixed catalogue.

That makes us a good fit if you're a bakery, patisserie, cake maker, doughnut shop or café that sells baked goods to take away and buys packaging in volume. Send us your sizes — or a photo of what you currently use — and we'll come back with pricing, usually the same day.

Custom branded bakery packaging is available too. See our custom packaging page for the printed side of the range.

Bakery Packaging We Source

The range UK bakeries typically buy from us. If your product needs a size or format not covered here, ask — we source across the trade.

01

Cake Boxes

Standard and deep cake boxes across the usual 6", 8", 10" and 12" footprints, plus tall boxes for layered and celebration cakes. Plain white, kraft and windowed.

02

Cupcake & Slice Boxes

Single, four-hole, six-hole and twelve-hole cupcake boxes with inserts, plus individual cake slice and dessert boxes for counter sales.

03

Pastry & Donut Boxes

Flat pastry boxes, donut boxes in half-dozen and dozen formats, and cookie or brownie boxes. Kraft and white board, windowed options available.

04

Bakery Bags & Greaseproof

Grease-resistant bakery bags, bread and baguette bags, and greaseproof sheets and wraps. Windowed bags for viscosity-free display of the product.

05

Boards, Drums & Inserts

Cake drums and boards in round and square, thin boards for slices, plus the inserts and pads that stop product moving in transit.
