Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the minimum order quantities?

Minimum order quantities vary by product and are confirmed on request. Contact us with your requirements and we'll advise on what's achievable for your product and budget.

Do I need print-ready artwork?

It helps if you have a logo file in a standard format (AI, EPS, PDF, or high-res PNG). If your artwork needs adjusting, we can advise on what's needed before production.

How long does it take?

Production and shipping timelines vary. As a general guide, allow several weeks from order confirmation to delivery. We'll give you a realistic timeline when we quote.

I'm a small independent café — is custom packaging viable for me?

Yes. We work with businesses of all sizes, including single-site independents. Contact us with your volume and we'll advise honestly on whether custom packaging is viable at your scale.
