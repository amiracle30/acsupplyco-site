Cup Types

Types of Coffee Cup We Supply

Single-Wall Cups

Standard disposable cups suitable for hot drinks. Lightweight and cost-effective for high-volume use. Available in all standard sizes.

Double-Wall Cups

Insulated cups that keep drinks hot without needing a sleeve. Suitable for cafés where presentation and customer comfort matter.

Cup Lids

Flat and domed lids to fit standard cup sizes. Secure fit, suitable for takeaway and sit-in service.

Cup Sleeves

Kraft and custom-print sleeves for single-wall cups. A cost-effective way to brand your cups without full custom print.

Custom Printed Cups

Cups printed with your logo or brand design. Available for cafés and businesses of all sizes — contact us to discuss minimum quantities.
