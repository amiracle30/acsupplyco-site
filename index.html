Our Services

What We Do

We manage the sourcing and supply process end to end — from finding and vetting manufacturers to coordinating import and delivery to UK buyers.

Product Sourcing

We work directly with international manufacturers to source the products our buyers need. We handle supplier communication, confirm specifications, and manage pricing negotiations so buyers don't need to deal with overseas suppliers themselves.

Wholesale Supply

We supply packaging and commercial consumables to UK trade buyers at wholesale prices. Orders are handled directly — no unnecessary layers, no inflated margins. We supply businesses that buy regularly and those placing one-off orders alike.

Import & Distribution

We coordinate freight and import logistics from international suppliers through to UK delivery, working with established forwarding partners. We manage the process so our buyers receive their goods without needing to handle the import side themselves.
